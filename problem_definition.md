# Problem Definition — FinSage

## Domain
Personal Finance Management for Indian salaried professionals and students.

## Target Users
- Salaried employees (₹3L–₹25L annual income)
- College students beginning to save/invest
- First-time earners with no financial advisor access

## Real-World Problem
80% of Indian earners have no access to a personal financial advisor.
They rely on WhatsApp forwards, YouTube, or random blogs — all of which
give generic, often wrong, non-personalized advice.

## Why Current Solutions Fail
| Tool          | Failure Point                                      |
|---------------|----------------------------------------------------|
| Google Search | Generic, not personalized, overwhelming            |
| Finance Apps  | Dashboard only, no conversation, no reasoning      |
| Chatbots      | Hallucinate, no memory, no tool use, no evaluation |
| Human Advisor | Expensive, inaccessible, slow                      |

## What FinSage Solves
- Answers finance questions from a verified knowledge base (no hallucination)
- Performs real calculations (SIP, EMI, tax, compound interest)
- Remembers context across a conversation
- Admits when it does not know
- Deployable as a web app — no installation needed
